Analyze electricity production and consumption in Finland

Welcome to our website! Here you can easily draw a line chart of Finland's electricity production, consumption and exportation by pressing the 'Overview'. You can also draw a line chart of Finland's electricity production compared to the production of wind, hydro and solar energy in Finland by pressing the "Renewability".

Remember to also select the starting and ending dates! Default setting is from yesterday 02:00 to today's current time (15 minutes before the current time of Finland). But you may select for example last year's summer months or winter months for comparison!

We hope you liked our website! Our website uses fingrid.fi as the source of the data and is able to fetch up-to-date data of Finland's electricity!
